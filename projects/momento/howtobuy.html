    • How to buy

    1. Download metamask wallet and add it as an extension on your browser.

    2. Send Ethereum (ETH) to your metamask wallet from binance/Coinbase/any other wallet that you have.

    3. Copy the contract address for our token and paste it on uniswap. (Make sure that you take the contract address only from our official channels)

    4. Swap the amount of ETH you wish to buy for against our token. (Set slippage to 12% and make sure you keep a little bit of ETH left for gas fess).
