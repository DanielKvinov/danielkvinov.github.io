    • About

    Momento is a project that aims to showcase some of the most interesting and fascinating moments of the past decade. It aims to bring about a feeling of nostalgia to people through art. We will have two categories of NFTs that we’d be showcasing through our project. One would be a general category where we’ll depict some of the most talked about moments of the past decade and the other one would be a category that solely belongs to some of the most prominent crypto projects out there and we aim to show some of the most iconic moments of their crypto journey in a single piece of art.

    Apart from this we’ll have a lot of DEFI features as well which would be mentioned as part of the tokenomics so basically you could say that this is a DEFI + NFT project. It’s going to be best of both worlds for our community and we aim to make it a community driver project.
